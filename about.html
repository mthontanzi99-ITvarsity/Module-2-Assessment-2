About Us

Located just of the N4 highway in eMalahleni (Witbank), Highveld Mall offers a comprehensive shopping destination catering to the needs of shoppers from as far afield as Middelburg, Belfast, Ogies, Kriel and Swaziland.
The mall sports over 185 stores including a full complement of national retailers, line stores, entertainment, services and a modern commuter facility that links directly to a main entrance via a covered walkway.

The mall attracts 8 million shoppers per year and employs over 3000 people making the "mall with more" an important social centre in this energetic mining and business community. At 85,000 m2 Highveld Mall, interlinked with the Tsogo Sun managed Ridge Casino and Entertainment Resort , offers a comprehensive shopping and entertainment destination.
